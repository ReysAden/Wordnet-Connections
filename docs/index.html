Connections

Find the common hypernyms of two words. The deeper you go, the more points you earn.

How to Play

You'll be given two words. Your goal is to find as many of their common hypernyms as possible in Wordnet's hypernym tree.

  • Type a hypernym and hit enter to guess
  • The deeper the hypernym, the more points it's worth
  • After your first guess, the intended meaning of each word is revealed
  • Find all hypernyms before you run out of guesses to win
